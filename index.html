Brief Biography

    Dr. Xiaofeng Cao is currently an Associate Professor at School of Artificial Intelligence in Jilin University(JLU). He received the PhD degree from Australian Artificial Intelligence Institute(AAII), University of Technology Sydney (UTS), and supervised by Prof. Ivor W. Tsang, where the AAII was ranked Top#1 AI center in Australia, and Top#10 in the world, and the CS of UTS was ranked top #11 in ARWU 2021.
